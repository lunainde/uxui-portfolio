about

⦿ Born to fly
⦿ German by jus Sanguines
⦿ Grew up in many countries
⦿ Found in Berlin, Germany

Passionate about design & innovation, fascinated by topics such as UX|UI, Ai, AR|VR|MR, Blockchain, NFTs, Cryptocurrency, Token Economy, Web3.0, Metaverse, DAOs, Renewable Energy -> with humanity in mind.

I have a background in architecture, and about 12 years of experience in interiors [beach clubs, hotels, resorts & high-end residentials]. I am a self-taught photographer, art lover, active traveler, and foodie by heart. I focus on brand & ux|ui design unlocking the values of businesses or personal images, to create that visual element that tells the story.

Also, I'm quite curious and interested in new ideas and new ways of doing things making life easier and pleasant. I believe design happens where style, functionality, and technology go hand in hand creating remarkable experiences for the people.

I believe we come to earth for good, to be served by machines giving each human being the choice to follow their true passion. Therefore, we are here to make the best out of our capacity to solve problems, dream, create, to automate tedious tasks like driving, provide free education for all globally, to advance, live well, and to enjoy life.
